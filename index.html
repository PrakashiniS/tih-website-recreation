About

Find Out More About Us

A hub for creativity and innovation, providing access to the latest technology and equipment to students.
TIH will produce in-house projects for our college as well as consultancy projects.
Project-Based Learning will be given to students, which will make them industry-ready.

WHY TIH ?

To provide a space to students to explore their passion, collaborate with their peers, and develop the skills they need to succeed in their chosen field. Bridging the gap between academia and industry, and facilitating the transfer of technology, knowledge, and resources.

MISSION

To promote innovation and entrepreneurship among students by providing a nurturing environment that encourages ideation, experimentation, and problem-solving. To facilitate interdisciplinary research and foster cross-functional collaborations, encouraging the exchange of ideas and expertise among different engineering disciplines. To continuously adapt and evolve in response to emerging technologies and industry trends, bridging the gap between academia and industry, and facilitating the transfer of technology, knowledge, and resources.

  • VISSION

    To become a leading Technology Innovation Hub across the globe for students by fostering a culture of creativity, collaboration, and cutting-edge technological advancements.

TIH Outcomes


  • Students will get project-based learning
  • Inhouse and consultancy projects will be done from hub
  • Trained students will get product-based company offer with higher CTC
